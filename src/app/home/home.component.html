Welcome to the Music Theory Reference Guide.

This is a collection of music theory tools to help with working out scales, chords and a whole lot more

Pages

  • Chords - calculator for all the main chords with any root note

  • Circle of Fifths
  • Terminology - brush up on your Italian ;)
